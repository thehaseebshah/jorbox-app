## Questions & Answers

### 1. **How many teeth does an adult great white shark have?**
**Answer: Approximately 300 teeth**
- Great whites have 5 rows of about 46 teeth each
- They continuously replace lost teeth throughout their lives
- **Source:** National Geographic, "Great White Shark Facts"

### 2. **What is the top running speed of a cheetah in miles per hour?**
**Answer: 70 mph**
- Cheetahs can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3 seconds
- Top speed sustained for only short distances (about 500 yards)
- **Source:** Smithsonian's National Zoo & Conservation Biology Institute

### 3. **How many heartbeats per minute does a hummingbird have at rest?**
**Answer: 250-500 beats per minute**
- During flight, heart rate can exceed 1,200 bpm
- Smallest hummingbirds have faster heart rates
- **Source:** Audubon Society research data

### 4. **What is the wingspan of a wandering albatross in feet?**
**Answer: 11-12 feet**
- Largest wingspan of any living bird
- Can fly for hours without flapping wings
- **Source:** British Antarctic Survey

### 5. **How many individual ants are estimated to exist on Earth?**
**Answer: 10 quadrillion (10,000,000,000,000,000)**
- Total biomass of ants equals that of humans
- Approximately 1 million ants per human
- **Source:** Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), 2022 study

### 6. **What is the gestation period of an African elephant in months?**
**Answer: 22 months**
- Longest gestation period of any land mammal
- Calves weigh about 200 pounds at birth
- **Source:** World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

### 7. **How deep can a sperm whale dive in feet?**
**Answer: 3,280 feet (1,000 meters)**
- Can hold breath for up to 90 minutes
- Deepest diving mammal in the world
- **Source:** National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

### 8. **What is the lifespan of a Galapagos giant tortoise in years?**
**Answer: 100+ years (oldest recorded: 152 years)**
- Slow metabolism contributes to longevity
- Some individuals may live over 170 years
- **Source:** Galapagos Conservancy

### 9. **How many pounds of bamboo does a giant panda eat per day?**
**Answer: 26-84 pounds**
- Spends 12-16 hours daily eating
- 99% of diet is bamboo
- **Source:** Smithsonian's National Zoo

### 10. **What is the jumping height of a red kangaroo in feet?**
**Answer: 10 feet high**
- Can also jump 25 feet horizontally
- Uses tail for balance during jumps
- **Source:** Australian Museum

### 11. **How many eggs can a queen bee lay per day?**
**Answer: 2,000 eggs per day**
- Can lay up to 1 million eggs in her lifetime
- Lives for 2-3 years on average
- **Source:** American Beekeeping Federation

### 12. **What is the body length of a blue whale in feet?**
**Answer: 98 feet (30 meters)**
- Largest animal ever known to have existed
- Heart alone weighs 400 pounds
- **Source:** World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

### 13. **How many muscles are in an elephant's trunk?**
**Answer: 40,000 muscles**
- Trunk contains no bones
- Can lift objects up to 770 pounds
- **Source:** Smithsonian Magazine

### 14. **What is the flying speed of a peregrine falcon in a dive (mph)?**
**Answer: 240 mph**
- Fastest animal in the world when diving
- Level flight speed: 40-60 mph
- **Source:** Cornell Lab of Ornithology

### 15. **How many scent receptors does a bloodhound have?**
**Answer: 300 million scent receptors**
- Humans have only 6 million
- Can follow trails over 300 hours old
- **Source:** American Kennel Club

### 16. **What is the tongue length of a giraffe in inches?**
**Answer: 18-20 inches**
- Prehensile tongue can grip branches
- Dark color prevents sunburn
- **Source:** San Diego Zoo Wildlife Alliance

### 17. **How many pounds can a bald eagle lift?**
**Answer: 4-5 pounds**
- Can lift about 1/3 of its own body weight
- Females are larger than males
- **Source:** U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service

### 18. **What is the hibernation period of a groundhog in months?**
**Answer: 3-5 months**
- Body temperature drops to 38Â°F during hibernation
- Heart rate slows from 80 to 5 beats per minute
- **Source:** National Wildlife Federation

### 19. **How many gallons of water can a camel drink in 15 minutes?**
**Answer: 30 gallons**
- Can survive without water for weeks
- Stores fat (not water) in humps
- **Source:** San Diego Zoo Wildlife Alliance

### 20. **What is the swimming speed of a sailfish in mph?**
**Answer: 68 mph**
- Fastest fish in the ocean
- Uses sail-like dorsal fin for herding prey
- **Source:** Florida Museum of Natural History

### 21. **How many times per minute does a bee flap its wings?**
**Answer: 230 times per second (13,800 per minute)**
- Creates characteristic buzzing sound
- Can fly up to 15 mph
- **Source:** University of Illinois Bee Research

### 22. **What is the weight of a male polar bear in pounds?**
**Answer: 900-1,600 pounds**
- Largest land carnivore
- Females weigh 330-650 pounds
- **Source:** Polar Bears International

### 23. **How many years can a parrot live in captivity?**
**Answer: 50-100 years**
- Larger species live longer
- African grey parrots can live 80+ years
- **Source:** Avian Welfare Coalition

### 24. **What is the venom yield of a king cobra in milligrams?**
**Answer: 200-500 mg per bite**
- Enough to kill 20 humans or 1 elephant
- Can strike from 8 feet away
- **Source:** World Health Organization (WHO)

### 25. **How many pounds of food does a grizzly bear eat per day before hibernation?**
**Answer: 90 pounds**
- Gains 3-6 pounds daily in fall
- Eats 20,000 calories per day during hyperphagia
- **Source:** National Park Service
