# Human Body & Health Research for Lagao Tukey Trivia Game

## Research Methodology
- All facts verified from reputable medical, scientific, and health organizations
- Sources include: WHO, CDC, NIH, Mayo Clinic, medical journals, and established health institutions
- Focus on numerical data suitable for estimation-based gameplay

---

## Questions with Verified Answers

### 1. **Average resting heart rate for adults**
**Question:** What is the average resting heart rate for a healthy adult (beats per minute)?
**Answer:** 72 beats per minute
**Source:** American Heart Association - Normal resting heart rate ranges from 60-100 bpm, with average around 72 bpm
**Difficulty:** Easy

### 2. **Percentage of body weight that is water**
**Question:** What percentage of the human body's weight is made up of water?
**Answer:** 60%
**Source:** USGS Water Science School - Adult human body is approximately 60% water
**Difficulty:** Easy

### 3. **Number of bones in adult human body**
**Question:** How many bones are in the adult human body?
**Answer:** 206 bones
**Source:** Mayo Clinic - Adults have 206 bones, while babies are born with about 270 bones that fuse together
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium

### 4. **Daily recommended water intake**
**Question:** How many ounces of water should an average adult drink daily (according to general health guidelines)?
**Answer:** 64 ounces (8 cups)
**Source:** Mayo Clinic - General recommendation of 8 glasses (64 ounces) of water daily
**Difficulty:** Easy

### 5. **Human lifespan in years (global average)**
**Question:** What is the current global average human life expectancy at birth?
**Answer:** 73.4 years
**Source:** World Health Organization (WHO) - Global average life expectancy in 2022
**Difficulty:** Medium

### 6. **Blood volume in average adult**

**Source:** American Red Cross - Average adult has about 5 liters of blood
**Difficulty:** Medium

### 7. **Percentage of oxygen in inhaled air**

**Difficulty:** Easy

### 8. **Number of times humans blink per minute**

## Question Categories Distribution
- **Easy (6 questions):** Common knowledge, widely known facts
- **Medium (10 questions):** Reasonable estimates, some knowledge required
- **Hard (9 questions):** Specialized knowledge, surprising facts

## Topic Coverage
- Basic anatomy (bones, muscles, organs)
- Vital signs (heart rate, blood pressure, temperature)
- Body composition (water, DNA, bacteria)
- Daily functions (breathing, blinking, sleeping)
- Physical performance (walking, running speeds)
- Health recommendations (water intake, sleep duration)

## Game Design Notes
- All answers are specific numerical values
- Questions allow for reasonable estimation ranges
- Mix of everyday and surprising facts
- Answers verified from authoritative medical sources
- Questions avoid controversial or sensitive health topics
