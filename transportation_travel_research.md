## Questions with Verified Answers

### 1. **What is the length of the Great Wall of China?**
**Answer:** 13,171 miles (21,196 km)
**Source:** UNESCO World Heritage Centre, Chinese State Administration of Cultural Heritage
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Includes all sections, walls, trenches, and natural barriers

### 2. **How many passengers does Dubai International Airport handle annually?**
**Answer:** 87 million passengers (2023)
**Source:** Dubai Airports official statistics, Airports Council International
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** One of the world's busiest international airports

### 3. **What is the cruising speed of a Boeing 747?**
**Answer:** 570 mph (920 km/h)
**Source:** Boeing official specifications, Federal Aviation Administration
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Typical cruising altitude at 35,000 feet

### 4. **How many cars are produced worldwide annually?**
**Answer:** 68 million vehicles (2023)
**Source:** International Organization of Motor Vehicle Manufacturers (OICA)
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** Includes passenger cars and light commercial vehicles

### 5. **What is the length of the Panama Canal?**
**Answer:** 51 miles (82 km)
**Source:** Panama Canal Authority official website
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Includes both Atlantic and Pacific approaches

### 6. **How many daily riders does the New York City Subway have?**
**Answer:** 3.5 million daily riders (2023 average)
**Source:** Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) official statistics
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Pre-pandemic was 5.5 million

### 7. **What is the maximum speed of the Shanghai Maglev train?**
**Answer:** 268 mph (431 km/h)
**Source:** Shanghai Maglev official specifications, Guinness World Records
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** World's fastest commercial magnetic levitation train

### 8. **How many miles of paved roads exist in the United States?**
**Answer:** 2.7 million miles (4.3 million km)
**Source:** U.S. Department of Transportation Federal Highway Administration
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** Includes all public roads

### 9. **What is the fuel capacity of a fully loaded Airbus A380?**
**Answer:** 84,600 gallons (320,000 liters)
**Source:** Airbus official specifications
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** World's largest passenger airliner

### 10. **How many people travel through London Heathrow Airport daily?**
**Answer:** 220,000 passengers daily (2023 average)
**Source:** Heathrow Airport Limited official statistics
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Europe's busiest airport

### 11. **What is the length of the Trans-Siberian Railway?**
**Answer:** 5,772 miles (9,289 km)
**Source:** Russian Railways official data
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** Longest railway line in the world

### 12. **How many bicycles are in Amsterdam?**
**Answer:** 880,000 bicycles
**Source:** City of Amsterdam official statistics, Dutch Bicycle Council
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** More bicycles than residents (872,000 people)

### 13. **What is the average cost of a first-class ticket from New York to London?**
**Answer:** $6,500 USD (average 2023)
**Source:** Airlines Reporting Corporation, International Air Transport Association
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** Varies by airline and season

### 14. **How many containers can the largest container ship carry?**
**Answer:** 24,000 TEU (Twenty-foot Equivalent Units)
**Source:** Maersk Line, maritime industry reports
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** OOCL Hong Kong class vessels

### 15. **What is the depth of the English Channel at its deepest point?**
**Answer:** 560 feet (170 meters)
**Source:** UK Hydrographic Office, French Marine Data
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Located in the Hurd's Deep area

### 16. **How many taxis operate in Tokyo?**
**Answer:** 52,000 licensed taxis
**Source:** Tokyo Transportation Bureau, Japanese Ministry of Land, Infrastructure
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** One of the world's largest taxi fleets

### 17. **What is the length of the Golden Gate Bridge?**
**Answer:** 8,980 feet (2,737 meters) total length
**Source:** Golden Gate Bridge Highway and Transportation District
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Notes:** Main span is 4,200 feet

### 18. **How many miles does the average American drive annually?**
**Answer:** 13,500 miles (21,700 km)
**Source:** U.S. Department of Transportation Federal Highway Administration
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** 2022 data for licensed drivers

### 19. **What is the maximum speed of a Formula 1 car?**
**Answer:** 231 mph (372 km/h)
**Source:** FIA (Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile) official records
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** Record set by Valtteri Bottas at Mexican Grand Prix 2016

### 20. **How many cruise ships are operating worldwide?**
**Answer:** 323 ocean-going cruise ships (2023)
**Source:** Cruise Lines International Association (CLIA)
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** Ships with capacity 100+ passengers

### 21. **What is the length of the Channel Tunnel (Chunnel)?**
**Answer:** 31.4 miles (50.5 km)
**Source:** Eurotunnel official specifications
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** 23.5 miles undersea

### 22. **How many flights take off from Atlanta's Hartsfield-Jackson daily?**
**Answer:** 2,700 flights daily (2023 average)
**Source:** Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport official data
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Notes:** World's busiest airport by passenger traffic

### 23. **What is the fuel efficiency of a Toyota Prius (average)?**
**Answer:** 57 MPG combined (2023 model)
**Source:** U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) fuel economy ratings
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Notes:** Hybrid electric vehicle

### 24. **How many people work for Uber worldwide?**
**Answer:** 32,000 employees (2023)
**Source:** Uber Technologies Inc. annual report
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Notes:** Excludes drivers who are independent contractors

### 25. **What is the circumference of Earth at the equator?**
**Answer:** 24,901 miles (40,075 km)
**Source:** NASA, International Earth Rotation and Reference Systems Service
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Notes:** Perfect for estimation games
