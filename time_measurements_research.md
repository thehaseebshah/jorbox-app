# Time & Measurements Research for Lagao Tukey Trivia Game

## Research Methodology
- All facts verified from reliable sources
- Questions designed for estimation gameplay
- Difficulty levels: Easy (1-8), Medium (9-17), Hard (18-25)
- Focus on estimable but not commonly known facts

## Easy Difficulty Questions (1-8)

### 1. How many seconds are in one day?
**Answer:** 86,400 seconds
**Source:** NASA - https://solarsystem.nasa.gov/resources/6847/how-long-is-a-day-on-earth/
**Verification:** 24 hours × 60 minutes × 60 seconds = 86,400

### 2. How many minutes are in one week?
**Answer:** 10,080 minutes
**Source:** Time and Date - https://www.timeanddate.com/time/weekduration.html
**Verification:** 7 days × 24 hours × 60 minutes = 10,080

### 3. How many feet are in one mile?
**Answer:** 5,280 feet
**Source:** National Institute of Standards and Technology - https://www.nist.gov/pml/weights-and-measures/metric-conversions
**Verification:** Standard US customary unit conversion

### 4. How many centimeters are in one meter?
**Answer:** 100 centimeters
**Source:** International Bureau of Weights and Measures - https://www.bipm.org/en/measurement-units/
**Verification:** Base SI unit definition

### 5. How many days are in a leap year?
**Answer:** 366 days
**Source:** Royal Observatory Greenwich - https://www.rmg.co.uk/stories/topics/why-do-we-have-leap-years
**Verification:** Calendar system with February 29th added

### 6. How many ounces are in one pound?
**Answer:** 16 ounces
**Source:** USDA Weights and Measures - https://www.usda.gov/
**Verification:** Standard US weight measurement

### 7. How many hours are in three days?
**Answer:** 72 hours
**Source:** Basic time calculation
**Verification:** 3 days × 24 hours = 72 hours

### 8. How many millimeters are in one centimeter?
**Answer:** 10 millimeters
**Source:** Metric system standards
**Verification:** Base-10 metric conversion

## Medium Difficulty Questions (9-17)

### 9. How many seconds are in one year (365 days)?
**Answer:** 31,536,000 seconds
**Source:** NASA - https://solarsystem.nasa.gov/resources/6847/how-long-is-a-day-on-earth/
**Verification:** 365 days × 24 hours × 60 minutes × 60 seconds = 31,536,000

### 10. How many kilometers are in one marathon?
**Answer:** 42.195 kilometers
**Source:** World Athletics - https://worldathletics.org/
**Verification:** Official marathon distance since 1921

### 11. How many minutes does it take for sunlight to reach Earth?
**Answer:** 8 minutes and 20 seconds (approximately 500 seconds)
**Source:** NASA - https://science.nasa.gov/sun/facts/
**Verification:** Distance 150 million km ÷ speed of light 300,000 km/s = 500 seconds

### 12. How many feet are in the height of the Statue of Liberty?
**Answer:** 305 feet (from ground to torch)
**Source:** National Park Service - https://www.nps.gov/stli/index.htm
**Verification:** Official monument measurement

### 13. How many seconds are in one hour?
**Answer:** 3,600 seconds
**Source:** Time standards
**Verification:** 60 minutes × 60 seconds = 3,600

### 14. How many meters are in one football field (American)?
**Answer:** 91.44 meters (100 yards)
**Source:** NFL - https://www.nfl.com/
**Verification:** 100 yards × 0.9144 meters/yard = 91.44 meters

### 15. How many days does it take the Moon to orbit Earth?
**Answer:** 27.3 days
**Source:** NASA - https://solarsystem.nasa.gov/moon/overview/
**Verification:** Sidereal orbital period

### 16. How many pounds are in one ton (US)?
**Answer:** 2,000 pounds
**Source:** NIST - https://www.nist.gov/pml/weights-and-measures
**Verification:** Standard US weight measurement

### 17. How many minutes are in one day?
**Answer:** 1,440 minutes
**Source:** Time calculation standards
**Verification:** 24 hours × 60 minutes = 1,440

## Hard Difficulty Questions (18-25)

### 18. How many nanoseconds are in one second?
**Answer:** 1,000,000,000 nanoseconds (1 billion)
**Source:** NIST - https://www.nist.gov/pml/weights-and-measures/si-units
**Verification:** SI prefix definition (nano = 10^-9)

### 19. How many seconds does it take for light to travel from the Sun to Earth?
**Answer:** Approximately 500 seconds (8 minutes 20 seconds)
**Source:** NASA - https://science.nasa.gov/sun/facts/
**Verification:** 150 million km ÷ 300,000 km/s = 500 seconds

### 20. How many meters are in one nautical mile?
**Answer:** 1,852 meters
**Source:** International Hydrographic Organization - https://iho.int/
**Verification:** International standard for maritime and aviation

### 21. How many microseconds are in one millisecond?
**Answer:** 1,000 microseconds
**Source:** NIST - https://www.nist.gov/pml/weights-and-measures/si-units
**Verification:** SI prefix conversion (micro = 10^-6, milli = 10^-3)

### 22. How many feet deep is the Mariana Trench at its deepest point?
**Answer:** Approximately 36,070 feet (10,994 meters)
**Source:** NOAA - https://www.noaa.gov/
**Verification:** Challenger Deep measurement

### 23. How many seconds are in one century (100 years)?
**Answer:** 3,153,600,000 seconds (excluding leap years)
**Source:** Time calculation
**Verification:** 100 years × 365.25 days × 24 hours × 60 minutes × 60 seconds ≈ 3.16 billion

### 24. How many astronomical units (AU) are in one light-year?
**Answer:** Approximately 63,241 AU
**Source:** NASA - https://science.nasa.gov/astronomy/light-year/
**Verification:** 1 light-year ÷ 1 AU (150 million km) = 63,241

### 25. How many Planck times are in one second?
**Answer:** Approximately 1.85 × 10^43 Planck times
**Source:** Max Planck Institute - https://www.mpg.de/
**Verification:** 1 second ÷ 5.39 × 10^-44 seconds (Planck time) = 1.85 × 10^43
