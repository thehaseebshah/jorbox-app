# Weather & Climate Trivia Questions for Lagao Tukey
*Research document with verified facts and sources*

## Questions (1-25)

### 1. By how many degrees Celsius has the global average surface temperature risen since pre-industrial times (approximate)?
**Answer:** 1.1 °C
**Source:** IPCC AR6 (global surface temperature change ~1.07°C for 1850–1900 to 2011–2020; rounded)  
https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/

### 2. What was the approximate atmospheric CO2 concentration in parts per million (ppm) in 2023?
**Answer:** 419 ppm
**Source:** NOAA Global Monitoring Laboratory (Mauna Loa)  
https://gml.noaa.gov/ccgg/trends/

### 3. By approximately how many centimeters has global mean sea level risen since 1900?
**Answer:** 21 cm
**Source:** NOAA sea level rise data  
https://www.climate.gov/news-features/understanding-climate/climate-change-global-sea-level

### 4. What was the world's total CO2 emissions from fossil fuels and industry in gigatonnes of CO2 in 2022 (approximate)?
**Answer:** 36.6 GtCO2
**Source:** Global Carbon Project  
https://www.globalcarbonproject.org/

### 5. What percentage of Earth's surface is covered by oceans?
**Answer:** 71% of Earth's surface
**Source:** NOAA / NASA Earth facts  
https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/facts/ocean-area.html

### 6. What is the record highest surface wind gust recorded on Earth (km/h)?
**Answer:** 408 km/h
**Source:** World Meteorological Organization (cyclone Olivia, Barrow Island, 1996)  
https://public.wmo.int/en

### 7. What is the magnitude of the largest earthquake ever recorded?
**Answer:** Magnitude 9.5 (1960 Valdivia earthquake, Chile)
**Source:** USGS earthquake history  
https://www.usgs.gov/

### 8. What is the approximate global average annual precipitation in millimeters?
**Answer:** 990 mm per year
**Source:** World Meteorological Organization / NASA Earth Observatory  
https://public.wmo.int/en

### 9. On average, how many named tropical cyclones form worldwide each year?
**Answer:** About 90 tropical cyclones per year
**Source:** WMO World Meteorological Organization statistics  
https://public.wmo.int/en

### 10. What percentage of Earth's freshwater is locked in glaciers and ice caps (approximate)?
**Answer:** ~68.7% of freshwater
**Source:** USGS (freshwater distribution)  
https://www.usgs.gov/special-topics/water-science-school/science/freshwater-storage

### 11. What was the global average concentration of methane (CH4) in the atmosphere in parts per billion (ppb) around 2022–2023?
**Answer:** ~1,900 ppb (1.9 ppm)
**Source:** NOAA Global Monitoring Laboratory  
https://gml.noaa.gov/ccgg/trends_ch4/

### 12. What is the estimated annual area of global forest loss in million hectares per year (recent decade average)?
**Answer:** ~10 million hectares per year
**Source:** FAO / Global Forest Resources Assessment  
https://www.fao.org/forest-resources-assessment/en/

### 13. What is the average speed of jet stream winds (km/h) commonly observed in the mid-latitudes?
**Answer:** Around 160–320 km/h (typical mean ~200 km/h)
**Source:** NOAA / meteorology references  
https://www.weather.gov/

### 14. What is the approximate volume of freshwater in all of Earth's lakes in cubic kilometers?
**Answer:** ~176,400 km³
**Source:** USGS / hydrology assessments  
https://pubs.usgs.gov/

### 15. Approximately how many heat-related deaths occur globally each year (recent estimates)?
**Answer:** Around 500,000 heat-related deaths per year (estimate varies by study)
**Source:** WHO / peer-reviewed studies on heat-related mortality  
https://www.who.int/

### 16. What is the typical pressure at sea level in millibars (hPa)?
**Answer:** 1013.25 hPa (standard atmospheric pressure)
**Source:** Meteorological standard atmosphere  
https://www.wmo.int/

### 17. What was the estimated global mean sea surface temperature in 2023 (°C, approximate)?
**Answer:** ~17.2 °C (global mean sea surface temperature around 2023)
**Source:** NOAA / NASA sea surface temperature products  
https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/

### 18. Approximately how many billion tons of plastic enter the oceans each year (recent estimates)?
**Answer:** ~8 million metric tons per year
**Source:** UN / scientific literature (varies by methodology)  
https://www.unep.org/

### 19. What is the approximate area of the Amazon rainforest in square kilometers?
**Answer:** ~5.5 million km²
**Source:** NASA / FAO / conservation resources  
https://www.nasa.gov/

### 20. How many years does a typical El Niño / La Niña cycle (ENSO) occur on average?
**Answer:** Every 2–7 years (typical average ~3–4 years)
**Source:** NOAA ENSO documentation  
https://www.climate.gov/

### 21. What fraction of global human-caused greenhouse gas emissions is CO2 from fossil fuels (approximate percent)?
**Answer:** ~75% of total greenhouse gas emissions (CO2 from fossil fuels and industry ~75%)
**Source:** IPCC / Global Carbon Project  
https://www.ipcc.ch/

### 22. What is the approximate depth of the ocean's average (mean) depth in meters?
**Answer:** ~3,688 meters (mean ocean depth)
**Source:** NOAA / NOAA National Ocean Service  
https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/facts/ocean-depth.html

### 23. Approximately how many people worldwide are currently living in areas that are expected to be below the mean sea level by 2100 under high-emissions scenarios (estimate)?
**Answer:** Tens of millions (estimate range 10–200 million depending on scenario and adaptation; commonly cited mid-range ~100 million)
**Source:** IPCC impacts assessment (wide uncertainty)  
https://www.ipcc.ch/

### 24. What is the typical concentration of atmospheric water vapor in the lower atmosphere by mass (approximate percent)?
**Answer:** ~0.25% by mass on average (varies widely by location)
**Source:** Atmospheric composition references / NOAA  
https://www.noaa.gov/

### 25. What is the current estimated global average annual temperature increase per decade (°C/decade) in recent decades?
**Answer:** ~0.18 °C per decade (varies by dataset and baseline)
**Source:** NOAA / NASA / IPCC trend analyses  
https://www.ipcc.ch/
