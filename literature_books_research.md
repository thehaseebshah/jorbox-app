## Questions with Verified Answers

### 1. Publication Year Questions

**Q1: In what year was J.R.R. Tolkien's "The Hobbit" first published?**
**Answer:** 1937
**Source:** HarperCollins publisher records, Tolkien Estate official website
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Reasoning:** Many people know it was pre-WWII but exact year is uncertain

**Q2: When was "Don Quixote" by Miguel de Cervantes first published?**
**Answer:** 1605 (Part 1)
**Source:** Biblioteca Nacional de España, Cervantes Institute
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Reasoning:** People know it's old but estimating 17th century vs 16th century is challenging

**Q3: In what year was "To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee published?**
**Answer:** 1960
**Source:** HarperCollins publisher archives, Library of Congress
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Reasoning:** Many know it's from the civil rights era but exact year uncertain
