# Entertainment & Media Research for Lagao Tukey Trivia Game

## Research Methodology
- All facts verified through reliable sources (Box Office Mojo, RIAA, Nielsen, Guinness World Records, etc.)
- Questions designed for estimation gameplay with clear numerical answers
- Spanning movies, TV, music, books, and video games
- Difficulty levels from easy to hard

---

## Questions with Verified Answers

### Movies & Box Office

**1. What is the worldwide box office gross of Avatar (2009) in US dollars?**
**Answer:** $2,923,706,026
**Source:** Box Office Mojo - https://www.boxofficemojo.com/title/tt0499549/
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Notes:** Highest-grossing film of all time (not adjusted for inflation)

**2. How many minutes long is the movie Titanic (1997)?**
**Answer:** 194 minutes
**Source:** IMDb - https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0120338/
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Notes:** One of the longest mainstream Hollywood films

**3. In what year was the first Star Wars movie (Episode IV: A New Hope) released?**
**Answer:** 1977
**Source:** Lucasfilm/Disney official records
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Notes:** Original theatrical release date

**4. How many Academy Awards did The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King win?**
**Answer:** 11 Oscars
**Source:** Academy Awards Database - https://www.oscars.org/

**Notes:** Tied for most Oscars won by a single film

**5. What is the worldwide box office gross of Avengers: Endgame in US dollars?**
**Answer:** $2,797,501,328
**Source:** Box Office Mojo - https://www.boxofficemojo.com/title/tt4154796/

**Notes:** Second highest-grossing film of all time

## Question Analysis for Game Balance

### Difficulty Distribution:
- **Easy (5 questions):** Questions about well-known dates, episode counts, or major releases
- **Medium (12 questions):** Specific numbers that require some knowledge but are reasonable estimates
- **Hard (8 questions):** Niche statistics or very specific figures

### Category Distribution:
- Movies & Box Office: 5 questions
- Television: 5 questions  
- Music Industry: 5 questions
- Books & Publishing: 5 questions
- Video Games & Digital: 5 questions

### Estimation Range Analysis:
- Smallest number: 11 (Oscars for LOTR)
- Largest number: 2.9 billion (Avatar box office)
- Most common range: Millions to hundreds of millions

## Game Design Notes

### Why These Questions Work for Lagao Tukey:
1. **Clear numerical answers** - No ambiguity
2. **Reasonable estimation ranges** - Players can make educated guesses
3. **Varied difficulty** - Mix of easy and challenging questions
4. **Diverse topics** - Appeals to different knowledge areas
5. **Current relevance** - Mix of classic and contemporary entertainment

### Suggested Point Values:
- Easy questions: 100 points
- Medium questions: 200 points  
- Hard questions: 300 points

### Time Considerations:
- Average estimation time: 15-30 seconds per question
- Total game time for 25 questions: 15-20 minutes including scoring
