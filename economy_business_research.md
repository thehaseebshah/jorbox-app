## Estimation Rationale

These questions work well for Lagao Tukey because:
1. **Reasonable Range:** Most people can make educated guesses within order of magnitude
2. **Not Common Knowledge:** Exact answers require specific research
3. **Verifiable Facts:** All answers can be independently verified
4. **Varied Difficulty:** Mix of easier and harder questions maintains engagement
5. **Relevant Topics:** Economy and business affect daily life, making estimation meaningful
