# Sports Records Research for Lagao Tukey Trivia Game

## Research Methodology
- All facts verified from official sources and reputable sports databases
- Questions designed for estimation gameplay (reasonable to guess but unlikely to know exactly)
- Mix of difficulty levels across various sports
- Focus on numerical answers: scores, times, distances, years, quantities

---

## Questions and Verified Answers

### 1. Cricket - Highest Individual Test Score
**Question:** What is the highest individual score in a single Test cricket innings?
**Answer:** 400 runs
**Source:** ICC Cricket Records - Brian Lara (West Indies) vs England, 2004
**Difficulty:** Medium

### 2. Football - Most World Cup Goals
